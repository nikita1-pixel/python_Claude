Whenever 8–10 new concepts have been taught in a session, automatically create a fun exercise (unprompted) that tests ALL of them together. Don't ask — just drop it.

**Why:** Nikita wants active recall baked in. Hearing 10 things ≠ knowing 10 things. The exercise is the proof.

**How to apply:**
- Keep a mental count of concepts taught per session
- At 8–10, generate the exercise before moving on — no waiting for "test me"
- Make it fun: scenario-based, predict-the-output, spot-the-bug, build-a-thing — mix formats
- Score after Nikita answers (X/10), flag every miss as a weak point in the session log
- Don't reveal answers until Nikita has attempted all questions
